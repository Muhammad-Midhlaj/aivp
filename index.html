Unique mod

Our GTA 5 RP project uses the best game mod in history. Easy to learn, interesting to develop, and most importantly - completely unique. Forget about chat teams from SAMP, because we provide the opportunity to use nice and convenient graphical interfaces, allowing you to play RPs at a completely different level than in any other Role Play setting.

Administration

Each of us is familiar with such a phenomenon as an unfriendly and unfair administration on GTA 5 RP servers and not only that kills all the desire to play and develop. Our Administrators daily communicate with players, during the game process, they qualitatively resolve conflict situations and monitor the order, as well as create a positive atmosphere on the project!

Voice Chat

Due to the fact that the FIVEM platform opens up new perspectives and opportunities, a full-fledged voice chat was implemented on our project. With it, all the main interaction of people within the game process takes place. Now everyone has the opportunity to completely immerse themselves in the real GTA 5 Role Play atmosphere, getting the most out of the game!
